About 1805

As Ekiti's premier nightlife destination, Club 1805 offers an unmatched experience with state-of-the-art sound systems, mesmerizing light shows, and Nigeria's finest selection of beverages. Our venue features multiple themed areas, VIP lounges, and a massive dance floor that comes alive with top DJ performances.

Committed to safety and excellence, we maintain the highest standards to ensure every visit is exceptional. Whether you're celebrating or just enjoying the night, Club 1805 delivers unforgettable experiences.
